Life

Orem is one of the safest cities in the USA. With low crime and good pay the city is livable. Orem is close to Silicon slopes, where there is lots of tech companies. Housing has gotten more expensive over the last few years with lots of people moving here from California, so they can have more freedom and safety.

Economy

Having One of the biggest Universitys in the city and having BYU in neigboring Provo. Orem has lots of educated people living here. There are lots of high paying jobs in Orem, and many people live here and commute to silicon slopes for high paying jobs.
